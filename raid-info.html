Paradøx Raid Info

To raid with Paradøx in 40-man content, the following addons are required:

  • Core Loot Manager
  • Deadly Boss Mods
  • ThreatClassic2 (or equivalent)

Earning DKP:

  • Earn 5 DKP for showing up to raid on time
  • Earn 2 DKP per world buff unbooned during official unboon callouts
  • Killing bosses in MC adds up to 50 DKP
  • Killing bosses in BWL adds up to 100 DKP
  • Killing bosses in AQ40 adds up to 150 DKP
  • Killing bosses in Naxx adds up to 250 DKP
  • Attending guild-sponsored ZG/AQ20 awards 10 DKP each (20-man raids will use SR loot system)
  • You can earn up to 30 DKP per week by donating eligible consumables

*** Signing up for raids and failing to appear without giving an officer notice will result in a loss of 10 DKP. ***

Tank Hard Reserves

To continue gearing our tanks for progression, HRs will still exist. The item that is hard reserved will cost 3X the maximum DKP earned for that raid.

  • In MC, this is ONLY Band of Accuria and Onslaught Girdle (150 DKP each)
  • In BWL, it is ONLY Drake Fang Talisman (300 DKP).

Using Core Loot Manager (CLM)

  • We will be using minimum bid increments of 5. If you would like to bid by "spamming" the MS or OS buttons, please right click the CLM minimap icon, click "Configuration", and tick the checkbox for "Enable auto-update bid values". If you would prefer to manually type your upbids, keep it unticked.


  • The bidding window will look like this.


  • Ensure you do not have "Hide in Combat" ticked, so we can bid between bosses during basic trash.

  • Before raid, click the "Toggle Test Bar" and move the auction timer somewhere visible without covering your raid frames (especially for healers and decursers).

  • If you do not have enough DKP to up-bid, you CAN match the current bid by entering the same amount in the input box and pressing the MS/OS button. In the event of a tie, whoever has the highest "Roll" in the Roll column will receive the item.

  • There is an "anti-swipe timer of ten seconds that resets after every bid.
